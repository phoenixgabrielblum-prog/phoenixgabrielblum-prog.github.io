01 The Problem

Your programs are growing faster than your infrastructure can manage.

The shift toward risk-bearing specialty and primary care arrangements has eroded visibility into which provider receives credit for improving their members' health.

Without a centralized attribution management system, health plans are forced into manual reconciliation, resulting in persistent attribution conflicts and double payments.

Phase 01 — How it works

AttributionManagement

Continuously assign every member to the right provider and risk arrangement, with the logic, conflicts, and changes tracked in one auditable system.

  • Every member, every attribution in one view — attributed lives and flagged conflicts side by side
  • Conflicts detected automatically — duplicate, hierarchy and dual-incentive, surfaced before payment
  • Confidence-scored, prioritized flags so the highest-impact problems get tackled first
  • A complete, versioned audit trail behind every decision — defensible in any dispute
  • Plugs into the eligibility files you run today — no rip-and-replace

Phase 02 — How it works

ProviderPerformance

Compare cost and quality across every vendor and program on a single view, so you can scale what's working and rationalize what isn't.

  • Each vendor's live panel, with adds and drops flagged — no more black-box rosters
  • Member-level detail behind every assignment — demographics, diagnoses, visit density
  • The reasoning made transparent — every change traces to the rule that drove it
  • A shared, defensible audit trail — vendors see the same provenance your team does
  • Vendor distribution today; a full self-service portal on the roadmap

Phase 03 — How it works

PaymentReconciliation

Produce clean, validated member-month and attribution data — free of duplicates and overlaps — so every payment is accurate and defensible.

  • Validated member-months by vendor and program, every cycle
  • Each member-month mapped to its PMPM rate — expected payment calculated, not estimated
  • Overlaps and duplicate incentives caught before payment goes out
  • Locked, versioned period snapshots — your team and vendors reconcile against one record
  • Clean inputs for reconciliation — quality, risk and stop-loss adjustments stay where they live

How it fits into your stack

Radiance uses your contracts and your existing attribution logic to power its intelligence. It's purpose-built to sit alongside your systems — not rip-and-replace. Contract logic, eligibility, claims and enrollment all flow in untouched, so nothing in your stack has to move.
